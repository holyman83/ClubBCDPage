When & Where We Meet

Club BCD meets on the second Saturday of each month at Mammoth Lake. Our goal is to create a relaxed dive day where divers and families can spend time together, enjoy the lake, and support one another in the sport of scuba diving.

When Second Saturday of each month
Where Mammoth Lake, Lake Jackson, Texas
Who Divers, families, and those interested in the scuba community

Our Purpose

The vision for Club BCD is to provide a welcoming, safe, and respectful diving community where experienced divers, newer divers, and families can connect. We want to encourage local diving, support growing divers, and help families enjoy the outdoors together.

Club Rules

  1. Keep the environment family friendly.
  2. Be respectful to each other and to the property.
  3. Children must be supervised at all times.
  4. All minors diving must have an adult diving with them.
  5. Divers are responsible for their own training, equipment, dive plan, and personal safety.
  6. No alcohol.
